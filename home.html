MECHANICS

Explores the microscopic properties of matter, molecular motion, statistical physics, and the laws of thermodynamics.

Examines electric and magnetic fields, charge interactions, currents, and electromagnetic phenomena.

Covers periodic motion, mechanical and electrical oscillations, and wave behavior, including optics.

Investigates the behavior of particles at atomic and subatomic scales, including quantum mechanics, atomic structure, and nuclear physics.
